Deskripsi Project

Go-Healthy is a digital application designed to make it easier for users to manage their health, from monitoring physical activity, recording nutrition, to reminding medical consultation schedules. As a UI/UX Designer, my focus is on creating an intuitive, aesthetic, and easy-to-use interface, with a seamless user experience in mind. Meanwhile, as a Frontend Developer , I am responsible for translating those designs into responsive and functional code, ensuring that the app is accessible on a variety of devices with optimal performance. The project reflects a collaboration between design and development to deliver innovative and user-friendly health solutions.
